WiFi Method: Totem Software Update

The Totem Compass needs to occasionally communicate with our servers to provide the best navigation experience. Our servers provide your Totem with software updates, future satellite positions, and magnetic declination maps so your GPS and navigation will continue having high accuracy.

By following the process outlined below, you can save a WiFi network’s credentials onto your Totem. Afterward, your Totem will remember this WiFi network and be able to easily update itself anytime the network is in-range with a simple triple-click of the SOS Button.

You will only need to repeat this process if you want to save a new WiFi network to your Totem at some point in the future.

Connecting to Your Totem’s Hotspot

  1. Turn on the Totem Compass
  1. Enable the Hotspot
  1. Connect to the Hotspot

Step 1) Turn on the Compass

Press the Power Button to turn on your Totem Compass. Please ensure your Totems are at least 50% charged before beginning the update process.

Step 2) Enable the Hotspot

Triple click the SOS button on the back of the Totem Compass. The Totem will reboot and will automatically determine the best way to check for updates. If the Totem doesn’t recognize any nearby WiFi networks it will launch a Hotspot for you to connect to.

  • The LEDs in the Totem’s Halo will turn pink and rotate counter-clockwise when the Hotspot is ready for you to connect

Step 3) Connect to the Hotspot

Connect to the Totem Hotspot using your phone, tablet, or laptop. We recommend using a laptop for the best experience.

  1. Put your phone, tablet, or laptop into “Airplane” mode
  1. Turn on your WiFi and connect to the network named: mytotem_xxxx (the trailing xxxx in the network name will be replaced with the last 4 characters of your Totem’s device ID). There is no network password.
  1. Once connected, open any browser* and enter: 10.9.8.7 in the URL/address bar. Then load the page. *Android users: Firefox is not supported, please use Chrome instead
  1. If the page doesn’t load immediately, refresh the page. If it’s still not loading, verify your phone or tablet is connected to the Hotspot and refresh the page again.

Note: after connecting to the Totem HotSpot, your device may warn you that the HotSpot doesn’t have internet access. Please disregard that message. The Totem HotSpot is not designed to provide conventional internet access.

Step 4) Set the Preferred WiFi Network

  1. Click on the “Connect to Your Totem” button
    • Your device will now show a “twinkle” animation showing you are connected
  1. Click on the button to “Scan for WiFi Networks”
  1. Choose the desired network from the dropdown menu, then enter the network password
  1. Click the button to “Save” the WiFi network
    • Your compass LED ring will turn green to indicate the WiFi network has successfully been saved

Step 5) Initiate Update

Press the button to update your single compass.

If desired, you can update multiple devices at once. This prevents you from repeating steps 2-3 above on any other Totems.

  1. Turn on all Totems you wish to update and place them horizontally (flat) next to the Hotspot Compass
    • Totems must be within 1 meter of the Hotspot in order to update
  1. On your phone/tablet, click the “Update Nearby Totems” button
    • Your Totem will start pulsing a teal/blue color to indicate it is signaling other Totems to update
    • After 30 seconds the Hotspot will stop signaling nearby Totems and will reboot and perform an update on itself
  1. All Totems that receive the message will update

▶ Software Update Failure

Your Totem will gracefully exit in the event the software update fails. When the Totem is unable to update:

  1. The outer ring will blink Orange
  1. The software will discard any partially downloaded updates and will reboot using the previous software version

If your Totem fails to update please note it is likely due to:

  1. Inadequate battery levels
    1. Please ensure your Totem is at least 50% charged before attempting an update
  1. Your Totem lost connection to the internet during its update attempt
    1. Please ensure your internet connection is stable and the Totem is connected to the device with an internet connection (such as your phone, tablet, or computer)
